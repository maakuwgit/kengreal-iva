KENGREAL® (cangrelor) is the first and only IV P2Y12 inhibitor and was specifically designed for the PCI setting1

Pharmacology: >98% inhibition of platelet aggregation in whole blood impedance aggregometry3

  • Onset within 2 minutes of administration; offset within 1 hour after discontinuation1
  • Pharmacokinetics are not influenced by age, sex, or patient symptoms1
  • Clearance is independent of renal or hepatic function; deactivated rapidly in the circulation by dephosphorylation1
  • 22% relative risk reduction vs clopidogrel in periprocedural primary composite endpoint of death, MI, IDR, and ST at 48 hours (P=0.005)1,4
  • 38% relative risk reduction in secondary endpoint of stent thrombosis vs clopidogrel at 48 hours (P=0.01)1,4
  • In CHAMPION PHOENIX bleeding events of all severities were more common with KENGREAL than with clopidogrel
  • Patients receiving KENGREAL had no statistically significant increase in either GUSTO-defined severe bleeding or need for transfusion
