KENGREAL® (cangrelor) pharmacology

KENGREAL is specifically designed as an adjunct to PCI1,2

Infusion should be continued for at least 2 hours or the duration of the procedure, whichever is longer

KENGREAL does not require a several-day washout period

Because of its offset within 1 hour, patients who need CABG or a valve replacement after PCI can proceed to surgery soon after KENGREAL discontinuation.1,4
