Study Details2

  • Phase 1 study in healthy volunteers (N=9)
  • Dose: 30 μg/kg IV bolus + 4 μg/kg/min IV infusion
  • KENGREAL® (cangrelor) blood levels and platelet activity were assessed over 150 minutes by whole blood impedance aggregometry in response to 20 μM of adenosine diphosphate (ADP)

CHAMPION PHOENIX study design4

ACS=acute coronary syndromes; NSTE-ACS=non–ST-segment elevation ACS; SA=stable angina; STEMI=ST-segment elevation myocardial infarction.
Study Type Randomized, double-blind, double-dummy, phase 3 trial
Purpose Compare periprocedural thrombotic events with KENGREAL® (cangrelor) versus clopidogrel (300 or 600 mg, per standard of care) in patients needing PCI
Patient Population
  • N=11,145 (randomized patients)
  • N=10,942 (modified intention-to-treat)
  • All-comer patients requiring PCI who were P2Y12 inhibitor naïve
  • Similar patient population as that seen in PCI practice (stable angina, NSTE‑ACS, STEMI)5,6
Key Inclusion Criteria
  • SA/NSTE-ACS: Confirmed need for PCI (by angiography)
  • STEMI: Intent for emergent PCI
Key Exclusion Criteria
  • Treated with an oral P2Y12 inhibitor in the past 7 days
  • Planned GP IIb/IIIa inhibitor use
